About Our Club

Innovation, Learning, and Excellence in Robotics

Welcome to the RTM Al Kabir Technical University Robotics Club, a premier student organization under the Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering. We are a passionate community of innovators, engineers, and technology enthusiasts dedicated to advancing robotics and automation.

Our club serves as a dynamic platform where students can explore cutting-edge robotics technologies, participate in competitions, collaborate on innovative projects, and develop essential skills for the future of engineering.

From beginner workshops to advanced research projects, we provide comprehensive learning opportunities that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical implementation. Join us in shaping the future of robotics and automation technology.
